IGERT Related Research
My research is in the development of 2D and 3D models of human skin regeneration in vitro and in vivo to evaluate emerging drug-delivery systems. We established a novel method to covalently conjugate fluorescently-labeled keratinocyte growth factor (KGF) into the fibrin matrix during polymerization to be released by migrating keratinocytes during reepithelialization. Future work includes designing fibrin and polymer-based drug-delivery systems for the treatment of skin wounds and metastatic cancers.
